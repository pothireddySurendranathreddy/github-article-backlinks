Professional Profile

Dr. P. Surendranath Reddy is a highly skilled Orthopaedic Surgeon with over 14 years of clinical, surgical, and academic experience, specializing in Joint Replacement, Arthroscopy, and Trauma Surgery. He is known for patient-centric care, evidence-based practice, and a strong commitment to medical education.

He completed MBBS from SV Medical College, Tirupati (2006), MS Orthopaedics from Kurnool Medical College (2011), and a Fellowship in Joint Replacement from Srikara Hospital, Secunderabad (2015). He currently serves as Associate Professor of Orthopaedics at PMRIMS.
